About

I'm a biomedical engineer transitioning into regulatory and clinical medical writing.

My background includes biomedical research, medical device validation, and structured scientific writing, with a first-authored conference paper, a clinical device white paper, and hands-on regulatory document training. I'm currently developing a Clinical Study Report (CSR) to deepen my regulatory writing practice.

I'm drawn to this field because regulatory writing is an important and responsible discipline. It fills the gap between effective clinical drugs and devices and the people who need them. It demands both a deep understanding of clinical studies and fluency in regulatory frameworks, a combination I have been actively developing through my biomedical research foundation and regulatory writing training.
